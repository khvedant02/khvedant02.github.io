This page organizes my work by technical area so readers, recruiters, and collaborators can quickly find relevant publications and artifacts.

## Primary Research Themes

### Neurosymbolic AI
I build **neurosymbolic AI** systems that combine language models, logic, and structured knowledge to improve reliability and transparency in reasoning workflows.

- [A Neurosymbolic Fast and Slow Architecture for Graph Coloring](/publication/2025-acs-neurosymbolic-graph-coloring)
- [NeuroSymbolic Knowledge-Grounded Planning and Reasoning in Artificial Intelligence Systems](/publication/2025-ieee-neurosymbolic-planning)
- [Specifying goals to deep neural networks with answer set programming](/publication/2024-icaps-goals-asp)

### Agentic AI and LLM Reasoning
My **agentic AI** work studies how to coordinate fast and slow reasoning modules with metacognitive control, feedback, and memory.

- [Language Models Coupled with Metacognition Can Outperform Reasoning Models](/publication/2025-arxiv-metacognition)
- [SOFAI-LM Tutorial and Lab Materials](/talks/)
- [A Neurosymbolic Fast and Slow Architecture for Graph Coloring](/publication/2025-acs-neurosymbolic-graph-coloring)

### Reinforcement Learning Research and Planning
I work on **reinforcement learning research** and planning systems where symbolic constraints and editable specifications shape policy behavior.

- [Toward Neurosymbolic Reinforcement Learning via Editable Specifications](/publication/2026-aaai-make-editable-specifications)
- [PDDLFuse: A Tool for Generating Diverse Planning Domains](/publication/2025-genplan-pddlfuse)
- [Towards Learning Foundation Models for Heuristic Functions to Solve Pathfinding Problems](/publication/2025-genplan-foundation-models)
- [Inductive Logic Programming for Heuristic Search](/publication/2025-ilp-heuristic-search)

### Knowledge Graph AI and Retrieval
I apply **knowledge graph AI** to retrieval and reasoning pipelines that need reproducibility and human control.

- [NeuroLit Navigator: A Neurosymbolic Approach to Scholarly Article Searches for Systematic Reviews](/publication/2025-arxiv-neurolit-navigator)
- [AI-Augmented Search for Systematic Reviews: A Comparative Analysis](/publication/2025-asist-ai-augmented-search)
- [GEAR-Up: Generative AI and External Knowledge-based Retrieval Upgrading Scholarly Article Searches for Systematic Reviews](/publication/2024-aaai-gear-up)

## Current Focus Areas

### Planning + Reasoning AI
I am currently focused on systems that jointly optimize planning, reasoning correctness, and adaptation under changing requirements.

### Neuro-symbolic Methods for Real-World AI
I target domains where pure end-to-end models are difficult to trust, including constrained decision-making, multi-step planning, and scientific workflows.

### AI Research Scientist Collaboration Areas
I am open to collaborations on:

- Agentic AI architecture design and evaluation
- LLM reasoning with symbolic verification
- Knowledge-grounded planning and constrained RL
- Benchmarks for robust planning and neuro-symbolic generalization
