I am **Vedant Khandelwal**, an AI Research Scientist and Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of South Carolina. I build AI systems that combine neural models and symbolic reasoning to make planning and decision-making more reliable, auditable, and adaptable.

## Research Focus

### Neurosymbolic AI and Knowledge-Grounded Reasoning
My research integrates large language models with symbolic constraints and structured knowledge. I design methods where knowledge graphs and logical rules guide reasoning quality, consistency, and explainability.

### Agentic AI and LLM Reasoning
I study **agentic AI** architectures for multi-step reasoning, metacognitive control, and error correction. My work includes fast-slow systems that combine efficient LLM generation with deliberate reasoning modules for hard tasks.

### Reinforcement Learning and Planning
I work on **reinforcement learning research** and planning, including neurosymbolic RL with editable specifications, foundation models for pathfinding, and logic-based heuristic learning for search.
